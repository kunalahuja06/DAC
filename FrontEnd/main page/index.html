FAQ's

Common FAQ's

What does IDAC stand for?


IDAC stands for "Ignite Digital Access Code"
These codes are unique serial numbers that provide address to a particular location

What are Plus Codes?


Plus Codes are like street addresses for people or places that don't have one.
Instead of addresses with street names and numbers, Plus Codes are based on latitude and longitude, and displayed as numbers and letters..

Why should you use IDAC?


We provide verified and accurate code for a given address and vice versa.
